Key Insights

Market Inefficiency Identified

Despite SBA pre-qualification offering clear benefits in terms of buyer interest and success rates, only 43% of eligible deals utilize this financing option. This represents a significant opportunity for improvement in deal structuring and seller education.

The Advertising Effect

Listings that advertise SBA pre-qualification in their titles receive 24-30% more buyer inquiries. This increased visibility translates directly into more potential buyers and competitive offers.

Time Trade-offs

While SBA deals experience 38 additional days under LOI due to diligence requirements, they close 54 days faster overall. This net time savings, combined with higher success rates, makes SBA financing advantageous for most eligible deals.

Deal Size Confounding

Initial analysis suggested higher commissions for SBA deals, but controlled analysis revealed this was due to deal size confounding - larger deals tend to use SBA financing while smaller deals use cash. When controlling for size, commission differences disappear.
