Key Finding

SBA pre-qualification creates a clear trade-off: listings with SBA pre-qualification achieve a 26% higher relative success rate (74.5% vs 59.1%) but take 43% longer to close (238 vs 166 days on average).

Important Context

Only 19.2% of listings qualify for SBA financing, making it a premium feature. The extended closing time may be worth the significantly improved success rate for sellers who can afford to wait.

Methodology & Data Quality

  • • Dataset: 531 listings with definitive outcomes (sold/lost) from 582 total CIMs

  • • Classification: Uses milestone IDs for accurate outcome determination (ID 7 = sold, IDs 8-9 = lost)
