💡 Why Don't Half Use SBA Despite Pre-qualification?
Possible Factors:
• Buyer preference: Cash buyers or those with existing financing relationships may opt out
• Speed to close: Alternative financing closes 54 days faster on average
• Competitive offers: In bidding situations, non-SBA offers may be more attractive to sellers
• Deal structure: Some deal terms may not align with SBA requirements
